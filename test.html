This vehicle has a 4 door saloon (sedan) type body with a front positioned engine that delivers power to the rear wheels. The Mark II 2.5 Tourer V is part of Toyota’s JZX100 range of cars. The 2.5 litre engine is a turbocharged, double overhead camshaft, 6 cylinder that produces 276 bhp (280 PS/206 kW) of power at 6200 rpm, and maximum torque of 378 N·m (279 lb·ft/38.5 kgm) at 2400 rpm. The engine transmits power to the wheels via a 5 speed manual transmission. It weighs a claimed 1480 kg at the kerb.
